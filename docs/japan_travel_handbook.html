Japan Travel Handbook

Day 1: Arrival in Tokyo

Arrive in Tokyo and check into your hotel. Visit Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa and explore the nearby Nakamise Shopping Street.

Day 2: Tokyo

Visit the Meiji Shrine, explore Harajuku and Takeshita Street, and visit the Tokyo Skytree for a panoramic view of the city.

Day 3: Tokyo to Kyoto

Take a Shinkansen to Kyoto. Visit Fushimi Inari Shrine and explore Gion District. Try a traditional Kaiseki dinner.

Day 4: Kyoto

Visit Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), explore Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, and visit Tenryu-ji Temple.

Day 5: Kyoto to Nara

Take a train to Nara. Visit Todai-ji Temple and see the Great Buddha. Explore Nara Park and interact with the deer.

Day 6: Nara to Osaka

Take a train to Osaka. Visit Osaka Castle and explore Dotonbori for dinner and nightlife.

Day 7: Osaka to Tokyo

Take a Shinkansen back to Tokyo. Visit the Imperial Palace Plaza and explore Akihabara for electronics and anime.

Special Proposal Location

Consider proposing at the top of the Tokyo Skytree or during a traditional tea ceremony in Kyoto.

Essential Japanese Phrases

  • Hello: こんにちは (Konnichiwa)
  • Thank you: ありがとう (Arigatou)
  • Excuse me: すみません (Sumimasen)
  • Yes: はい (Hai)
  • No: いいえ (Iie)

Travel Tips

  • Carry a portable charger for your electronic devices.
  • Learn basic Japanese phrases to enhance your experience.
  • Use public transportation for convenient travel.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
